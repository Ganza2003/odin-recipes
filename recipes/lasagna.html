lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù,\ béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400ºF
  2. Brown the beef and onion
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce
  4. Begin layering the lasagna
  5. Continue layering the lasagna
  6. Bake the lasagna for 1 hour
  7. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and finish baking
  8. Cool the lasagna for 15 minutes
